FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I stake with Intelly?

You can stake by using the Binance pay feature on the platform. Simply select the desired staking period and payout type and complete your payment.

When are the rewards distributed?

Each participant can select his individual staking period on the details page. The distribution date depends on the selected preferences of the user.

How are rewards distributed?

Rewards are distributed automatically after the selected time period has been completed and funds will be received directly in the Binance account. (INTL will be sent to users separate wallet)

What are INTL payouts?

If users select the option to be paid in INTL instead of USDT, they receive a 9% bonus on their payout. Or if USDT + INTL option is chosen, your initial investment amount will be paid in USDT, and the profit is paid in INTL with 4% increased APY.

How does the referral system works?

If you share your referral code with someone, you will receive %10 from all their staking profits. (You should have initiated a stake to receive automated payouts through Binance Pay. Contact us at intelly.tech/contact)

If you register with someone else's referral code, you will gain %2 extra for all the prespecified APYs on the platform. (applicable for the first 90 days, starting with the first staking)
